Weaponry

Meet Our Collection

The game offers a huge variety of weapons to choose from, from a trusty sidearm to full auto machine guns.

Assault Rifles

 

Assault rifles are arguably the most versatile guns on the playing field. Don't have a sniper? Pop that 6x scope on the assault rifle. Need a close-range gun? Use that red-dot reticle on the assault rifle.

 

Sub-Machine Guns

 

Light, powerful and compact, the sub-machine guns can absolutely shred through enemies in close-quarter combat. Using either 9mm or .45 APC ammo, these guns are a low-recoil replacement for ARs.

 

 

Designated Marksman Rifles

 

Designated Marksman Rifles, or DMRs for short, sits in between assault rifles and bolt-action snipers. They offer fast bullet velocity and a high firing rate, making it favourable for those that are not accurate with bolt action sniper rifles.

 

Shotguns

 

Functioning as great sidearms, shotguns are best for rushing into buildings, as a single shot is most likely to confirm a kill. Be aware, however, as long reload times and a few misses can be the difference between a kill and being killed.

 

 

Bolt Action Sniper Rifles

Doing the most single-hit damage in the game, sniper rifles are definitely the most powerful, but only if you land your shots. A single headshot with any sniper rifle to a level 1 or 2 helmet will guarantee you an instant kill.
